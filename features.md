# 💎 Fintracker - Features Overview

Fintracker is a premium, privacy-focused personal finance manager built for precision and clarity. It combines a sophisticated "Editorial Brutalist" aesthetic with powerful local-first tracking capabilities.

---

## 🚀 Onboarding & Setup
- **Guided Experience**: A seamless three-step flow (Welcome → Profile → Initial Account) to get you started in seconds.
- **Smart Localization**: Automatic currency detection based on your device locale.
- **Instant Productivity**: Pre-seeded with over 50+ common income and expense categories across Essentials, Transport, Health, Lifestyle, and more.

## 📊 Dashboard & Financial Pulse
- **Net Position**: Instant visibility into your total financial health.
- **Multi-Account Tracking**: Real-time balance across all your financial repositories.
- **Live Feed**: Quick access to your most recent transactions directly from the home screen.

## 📈 Advanced Analytics & Insights
- **Interactive Windows**: Filter your data across 7D, 30D, 90D, or All-Time ranges.
- **Practical Metrics**:
    - **Avg Daily Burn**: Know exactly what you spend per day.
    - **Savings Rate**: Track what percentage of your income you're keeping.
    - **Financial Runway**: See how many days your current balance lasts at your current spend rate.
    - **In/Out Ratio**: A clear ratio of income vs. expenses.
- **Comparative Intelligence**: Automatic period-over-period delta tracking for Income, Expenses, and Net Position.
- **Visual Flow**: Dynamic 7-day cash flow charts visualizing income against expenses.
- **Spending Breakdown**: Beautifully rendered top category analysis with relative progress tracking.
- **Allocation Logic**: Visualize how your tracked balance is split across different accounts.

## 💸 Transaction Management
- **Precision Logging**: Detail-rich transaction entry including notes, timestamps, and multi-entity linking.
- **Infinite History**: Fluid scrolling through your entire financial history.
- **Power Filtering**: Drill down by Transaction Type (CR/DR), specific Accounts, or Categories.
- **Gesture Control**: Intuitive swipe actions to edit or delete records quickly.
- **Integrity First**: Automatic reversal of account balance impact when transactions are modified or removed.

## 🛠️ Personalization & System
- **Dynamic Themes**: Full support for Light, Dark, and System-adaptive interface modes.
- **Visual Identity**: Customizable icons and color palettes for every account and category.
- **Local-First Privacy**: All data is stored securely on your device via SQLite. No cloud, no tracking.
- **Maintenance Tools**: Factory Reset option to completely purge local storage and start fresh.

---

## 🏗️ Technical Highlights
- **Architecture**: React Native + Expo + Expo Router.
- **Data Layer**: Drizzle ORM + SQLite (Expo SQLite).
- **Animation**: High-performance interactions powered by React Native Reanimated.
- **Typography**: Precision-engineered fonts: Bricolage Grotesque & JetBrains Mono.
